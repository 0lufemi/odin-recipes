Iyan

Description

Iyan (or pounded yam) is a delicacy common among the yoruba ethnic group of south west Nigeria.

It is a swallow food made from pounding cooked yam

Ingredients

  • Yam
  • Mortar and Pestle

Steps

  1. Cut the yam into medium sized cubes and peel the back
  2. Rinse the yam in clean water
  3. Put in boiling water and allow to cook till it is moderately soft
  4. Transfer boiled yam into Mortar and beat/pound till
    it's smooth and soft with Pestle
  5. Serve
