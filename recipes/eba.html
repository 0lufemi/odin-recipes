Eba

Description

Eba is a delicacy common among the yoruba ethnic group of south west Nigeria.

It is mostly called a swallow food and eaten with soups

Ingredients

  • Garri
  • Hot Water

Steps

  1. Put the hot water on fire
  2. Pour the quantity of garri enough for you in a bowl
  3. wait for the water to boil then pour in the bowl of gari
  4. Turn till it is soft and blended enough for consumption
