Efo Riro

Description

Efo riro is a delicacy common among the yoruba ethnic group of south west Nigeria.

It is mostly eaten with swallow foods like eba, semo, pounded yam etc.

Ingredients

  • Green vegetables
  • Oil
  • Locust beans (iru)
  • Scotch bonnet pepper
  • Smoked fish

Steps

  1. Pick the green vegetables
  2. Wash with lukewarm water
  3. Pour Oil in an empty pot on fire
  4. Pour locust beans after washing
  5. Add pepper mix and fish
